<h2>◈ About Me</h2>
</div>

I am a Computer Science student and software engineer with a strong focus on building production-grade AI-powered products. I approach engineering as a craft — writing clean, typed, maintainable code across the full stack, from database schema to user interface to deployed infrastructure.

My primary area of work is at the intersection of **AI systems** and **product engineering**. I build LLM-integrated platforms that solve real business problems — not demos, but deployed products with real users and measurable outcomes. My flagship company, **Socle**, is an AI intelligence platform for the luxury hospitality industry, combining sentiment analysis, trend detection, and automated outreach into a single enterprise-grade product.

I hold conviction that great software engineering is inseparable from product thinking. Every system I build is designed with scale, security, and user experience as first-class concerns from day one.
